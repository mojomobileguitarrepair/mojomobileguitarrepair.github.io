WE’RE GIVING YOU ALL THE MOJO WE GOT!

Mojo Mobile Guitar Repair was founded on the idea of providing quality instrument repair and the gear you need - delivered to your door. No need to leave your home - our pick up/drop off service is fast, efficient, and affordable. Instrument pick-up and return is always FREE within our service area!

For instrument restrings and set up, Mojo has all the best names in strings available, including Elixir, GHS, Fender, D'Addario, DR, Ernie Ball, and Dean Markley, and Augustine, to name a few.

Mojo also supplies all the accessories you need - instrument cases by TKL, Peavey, SKB, and more; guitar and bass straps by Levy's, Fender, D'Addario, and others; capos, slides, picks, mic and instrument cables and stands, effects pedals - everything you need to keep on rockin'! (Indicate what you need when you contact us and we'll follow up with info/quote. Online catalog coming soon!)

Whether you're in the garage, in the studio, or on stage, our passion is to ensure your instrument is playing at its best ... so you can perform at yours.

HERE'S HOW IT WORKS:

MOJO SETUP FOR ELECTRIC GUITARS (FLOYD ROSE STYLE)

​Mojo magic! This ain’t some kinda voodoo - a good setup is just what you need for the best tone and playability from your instrument.

Setup service for Floyd Rose style bridge (floating or non-floating) includes complete restring, instrument and fingerboard cleaning (fingerboard conditioning on Ebony and Rosewood boards), truss rod adjustment, string height (action) adjustment, intonation, reset bridge fine tuners, bridge spring tension adjustment, D-tuner calibration (if applicable), headstock tuner check (with tension adjustment if applicable), potentiometer and switch cleaning, and tuned to standard or your tuning preference. Strings extra.
